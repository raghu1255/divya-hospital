Department of General Medicine

General Medicine

The Department of General Medicine is usually considered the bedrock on which the plethora of other treatments served by super speciality hospitals is built. At Divya Hospital, the Department of General Medicine critically examines a patient's general health care and prescribes the next course of medical assistance to the patients.

Our General medicine department highly qualified and acclaimed team of doctors with experience in handling a comprehensive sphere of illnesses refer the patients to the required super-specialist department.

Our General medicine specialisation in:

  1. Clinical diagnosis
  2. Treatment of persistent health issues
  3. Maintaining adult wellbeing
  4. Prevention of diseases.
  5. Thyroid Disorders
  6. Hypertension
  7. Fevers
  8. Heart Disease
  9. Kidney Disease & Dialysis Follow up
  10. Anxiety & Stress Disorder
